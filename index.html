Regular expression mini-project.

User form

Username must be and contain 5 - 12 characters (underscore is valid)

Email must be a valid address, e.g. me@mydomain.com

Password must alphanumeric (@, _ and - are also allowed) and be 8 - 20 characters

Phone number must be a valid IN phone number (10 digits)

Slug must contain only lowercase letters, numbers and hyphens and be 8 - 20 characters
